# Observator — Video Recording Guide

---

**"Observator is the UAE's labour market intelligence platform..."**

Open Dashboard. Show hero KPI bar loading. Slow scroll down through the full page.

**"Every chart on this screen is backed by verified, traceable data..."**

Pause scrolling. Let the Dashboard breathe.

---

**"The Supply Side shows the full education pipeline..."**

Click "Supply Side" in sidebar. Scroll to enrollment trend area chart. Hover a data point.

**"Which emirates have the most students..."**

Scroll past emirate bars, gender donut, STEM ring.

**"Every institution ranked by programs and graduate output..."**

Scroll to institution ranking table. Hover top row.

**"...the actual skills each university produces..."**

Scroll to "Skills Produced by Education" bar chart. Pause.

---

**"The Demand Side captures the real job market..."**

Click "Demand Side" in sidebar. Scroll through monthly volume chart, top industries bars, experience donut.

**"Salary benchmarks across emirates and occupations..."**

Scroll to salary benchmarks table.

**"...the skills employers actually require..."**

Scroll to "Most In-Demand Skills" bar chart. Pause.

**"This is not a survey. This is what the market is doing."**

Stay still on the chart.

---

**"The Dashboard is where it all connects..."**

Click "Dashboard" in sidebar. Scroll to Skill Gap Intelligence section. Show 6 KPI cards.

**"This chart shows the same skills on both sides..."**

Scroll to butterfly chart. Hover a bar to show tooltip.

**"The heatmap groups skills by category..."**

Scroll to heatmap. Hover a dark pill.

**"On the left — skills the market demands but nobody teaches..."**

Scroll to Critical Gaps + Potential Surplus side by side. Pause.

**"Want to go deeper? Filter by skill, occupation, university, or region..."**

Scroll to Explorer section. Click "By Skill" tab. Select region dropdown → Dubai. Type "engineering" in search.

**"Click any row to drill into the detail..."**

Click a skill row. Detail panel expands — jobs left, courses right.

**"Switch views — compare institutions, programs, or occupations..."**

Click "By University" tab. Cards load.

**"And every single chart can be flipped to reveal its full data story..."**

Scroll up to any chart. Click the ℹ button. Chart flips to data story.

**"...with direct links into the Knowledge Base..."**

Click a source table link. Knowledge Base opens. Browse data rows.

**"Navigate across tables. Follow relationships..."**

Click a teal FK value in a row. Navigates to related table. Click back.

**"Full transparency for government-grade decisions."**

Stay on Knowledge Base.

---

**"Observator also maps AI disruption risk across the entire economy..."**

Click "AI Impact" in sidebar. Scroll to spider chart.

**"...theoretical AI capability versus what's actually being automated today."**

Hover spider chart nodes — blue vs gold.

**"Drill into any job family..."**

Scroll to taxonomy explorer. Click a family → click an occupation → skills with AI impact bars show.

**"So workforce planners can design reskilling programs ahead of displacement..."**

Stay on skill detail view.

---

**"And if you'd rather just ask a question — you can..."**

Click "Dashboard" in sidebar. Scroll to AI Research Assistant.

**"The AI assistant has access to the entire database..."**

Click a suggested question. Response streams in.

**"Toggle web search for live market intelligence..."**

Click Web Search toggle. Show citations under response.

---

**"A minister sees the gap..."**

Slow scroll Dashboard top to bottom.

---

**"Observator. See the gap. Close the gap."**

Fade to logo + tagline.
